Welcome Ken Collins!

CustomInk technology is very excited to announce that Ken Collins has joined the team!

Ken is better known online as metaskills and is an active open source contributor who currently maintains the rails MS SQL Server Adapter. More recently Ken has developed an interest in Javascript contributing submissions to Spine and creating PhantomJS Runners for Mocha.

Ken spent the week with us here in our Tyson's Corner headquarters, but he will continue to work from his Norfolk, VA home as a remote developer.
