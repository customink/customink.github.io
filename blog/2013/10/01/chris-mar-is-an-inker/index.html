Chris Mar is an Inker

01 Oct 2013
by Karle Durante

CustomInk technology is proud to announce that Chris Mar is our newest Inker! Following the new hire tradition, Chris pushed his first deploy to Booster.com within hours of arrival.

Chris joins us from Spree Commerce, where he worked as a core contributer to their open source e-commerce platform. Chris is also a co-organizer of Ruby LoCo, a Loudoun County, Virgina based Ruby user group. You can hear him every third Friday on the Ruby5 podcast.

If you missed Chris at Ruby DCamp last weekend, then be sure to look out for him at RubyConf this November.
